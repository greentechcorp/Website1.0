FAQs

  • What does the bin do?
    • This bin sorts your rubbish for you so you don't have to!
  • How does it work?
    • The technology the bin uses includes AI, WiFi sensors and weight sensors to scan your rubbish piece-by-piece and detect what type of material your rubbish is. From there, it's sorted into the correct bin and brought to get recycled!
  • How do I clean it?
    • Clean the bin every few weeks and check that there are no stains. Use a scrubber and spray if neeeded.
